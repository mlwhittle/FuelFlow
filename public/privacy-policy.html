FuelFlow Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 27, 2026

1. Introduction

FuelFlow ("we," "our," or "us") is a nutrition tracking and wellness application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application and web service.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Email address and display name when you create an account via Firebase Authentication or Google Sign-In.
  • Nutrition Data: Food logs, calorie counts, macro nutrient data, recipes, and meal plans you enter into the app.
  • Activity Data: Exercise logs, step counts, and fasting timer data you manually enter.
  • Photos: Food photos you take or upload for AI analysis. Photos are processed by Google Gemini Vision API for food identification and are not stored on our servers.
  • Community Posts: Content you share in the Community feed, including posts, likes, and comments.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide nutrition tracking, meal planning, and wellness features.
  • To generate personalized recommendations based on your logged data.
  • To enable community features (posting, liking, commenting).
  • To improve our app and services.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database (Firestore), and hosting.
  • Google Gemini Vision API: AI-powered food photo analysis.
  • USDA FoodData Central API: Food nutrition database lookup.
  • Open Food Facts API: Barcode product lookup.

These services have their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them.

5. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase services, which employ industry-standard encryption and security practices. Local app data (settings, preferences) is stored on your device using browser local storage.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data as described in the Third-Party Services section above, which is necessary for app functionality.

7. Your Rights

You may:

  • Delete your account and associated data at any time through the app Settings.
  • Export your data by contacting us.
  • Opt out of community features by not posting.

8. Children's Privacy

FuelFlow is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@fuelflow-app.com
